PermitsNeeded

Overview

PermitsNeeded is an antivirus that only counts on permissions.
For example, a messenger app that requires your camera and microphone will need to get your permission in order to access it with the help of PermitsNeeded.
This is a very simple and easy to use antivirus that will help you to protect your privacy.
